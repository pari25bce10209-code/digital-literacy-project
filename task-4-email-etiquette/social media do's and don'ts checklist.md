# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

| ✅ Do’s                              | ❌ Don’ts                              |
|-------------------------------------|----------------------------------------|
| Maintain a professional profile     | Share personal or sensitive information |
| Think before posting                | Post inappropriate or offensive content |
| Use privacy settings wisely         | Spread misinformation                  |
| Share useful and positive content   | Use abusive or disrespectful language  |
| Respect others online               | Accept unknown requests blindly        |
| Keep your information updated       | Overshare personal details             |
| Verify information before sharing   | Believe or forward fake news           |
