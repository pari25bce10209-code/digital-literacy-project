## Task Summary (with Module Connection)

### Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic  
This task is based on concepts taught in **Module 1 (Hands-on Training and Presentation Tools)**. I created an infographic using Canva to explain digital literacy, useful tools, safe internet practices, and email etiquette. This helped me apply design and presentation skills learned during the module.

🔗 Folder: `task-1-presentation/`

### Task 2: Digital Portfolio  
This task relates to **Module 2 (Building a Digital Portfolio)**. I created and updated my profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. This task helped me understand how to build a professional online presence and showcase my skills and projects.

🔗 Folder: `task-2-portfolio/`

---

### Task 3: Platforms Exploration  
This task is connected to **Module 3 (Programming and Collaboration Platforms)**. I explored coding platforms like HackerRank and tools such as Google Forms and Google Sheets. I also created a quiz to understand data collection and teamwork.

🔗 Folder: `task-3-platforms/`

### Task 4: Email Etiquette  
This task is based on **Module 4 (Email and Social Media Etiquette)**. I wrote professional emails and created a checklist of social media do’s and don’ts. This improved my communication skills and understanding of professional behavior online.

🔗 Folder: `task-4-email-etiquette/`

### Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness  
This task is related to **Module 5 (Cybercrime Awareness and Prevention)**. I created a case study on phishing based on a real-world incident and developed a prevention checklist. This helped me understand online threats and safety measures.

🔗 Folder: `task-5-cybercrime/`
