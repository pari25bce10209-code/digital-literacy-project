# Cyber Safety Checklist

## ✅ General Prevention Tips
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links  
- Always check the website URL before entering any details  
- Use strong and different passwords for each account  
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible  
- Never share your OTP, passwords, or personal details  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or important work  
- Keep your apps, devices, and antivirus updated  
- Be careful with emails that create urgency or offer rewards  

## 💳 UPI & Payment Safety
- Do not approve unknown payment requests  
- Always verify the receiver’s details before sending money  
- Avoid scanning random QR codes  

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930
